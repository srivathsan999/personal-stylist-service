Building a Capsule Wardrobe

By Sarah Jenkins • Oct 28, 2025

Sustainability starts in your closet. A capsule wardrobe is a curated collection of versatile pieces that you love to wear and that work together seamlessly.

The 30-Item Rule

A classic capsule wardrobe often consists of around 30 key items, excluding activewear and essentials. The goal is to focus on quality over quantity. Choose fabrics that last and silhouettes that don't go out of style.

Start with the Basics

Every capsule needs a strong foundation. This includes a perfectly fitted blazer, high-quality denim, a versatile white shirt, and a neutral sweater. These pieces are the building blocks of dozens of outfits.

"Style is a way to say who you are without having to speak."

Color Coordination

Stick to a base palette of neutrals like black, navy, camel, or grey. Then, add 2-3 accent colors that complement each other. This ensures that almost every top in your capsule will match almost every bottom.
