Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical styling session take?

A standard styling session typically lasts 2-3 hours, depending on the service. Wardrobe detox sessions may take 4-5 hours, while personal shopping experiences can range from 3-6 hours based on your needs.

Do you work with specific budgets?

Absolutely! We work with clients across all budget ranges. During our initial consultation, we'll discuss your budget and create a customized plan that maximizes value while achieving your style goals.

Can you help with virtual styling?

Yes! We offer comprehensive virtual styling services via video calls. This includes wardrobe audits, personal shopping with online retailers, and style consultations from anywhere in the world.

What should I prepare for my first session?

Come with an open mind and any inspiration images you love! For wardrobe sessions, have your closet accessible. We'll provide a detailed questionnaire beforehand to understand your lifestyle and style preferences.

Do you offer ongoing style support?

Yes! We offer monthly retainer packages that include ongoing style advice, seasonal wardrobe updates, and priority access to new collections. Perfect for busy professionals who want continuous style support.
