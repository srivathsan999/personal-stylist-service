Mastering Color Theory

By Sarah Jenkins • Nov 05, 2025

Color is one of the most powerful tools in styling. Understanding how to mix and match colors can elevate your look from ordinary to exceptional.

The Color Wheel

Begin by understanding complementary colors—those opposite each other on the wheel. Think navy and camel, or burgundy and forest green. These combinations create a balanced, high-contrast look that always feels polished.

Monochrome Magic

Dressing in various shades of the same color is a foolproof way to look taller and more sophisticated. The trick is to mix textures—pair a wool sweater with silk trousers in the same tonal family to add depth.

"Colors, like features, follow the changes of the emotions."

Understanding Undertones

Are you warm or cool? Choosing colors that match your skin's natural undertone is the difference between looking radiant and looking washed out. Warm undertones shine in earthy colors, while cool undertones pop in jewel tones.
